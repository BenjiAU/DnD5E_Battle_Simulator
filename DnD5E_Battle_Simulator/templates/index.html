Welcome to the battle simulator, inspired by the Wizards of the Coast TTRPG and the show Critical Role.
This is a programming exercise designed to strengthen my Python skills, and help answer questions about 'Who would win' in certain iconic Critical Role battles if they were to be replayed or realised
It all started with the question; at what range can a max-level Percy bring down a max-level Grog? The answer might surprise you!
You can currently configure which combatants should battle from the predefined list, as well as change their starting co-ordinate positions and 'Team'
Creatures on the same team will not attack each other, and victory will be declared for one Team when all members of enemy teams have been vanquished.
There will be bugs and things that are unfinished; that's part of the fun!
