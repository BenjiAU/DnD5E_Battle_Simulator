Welcome to the battle simulator, inspired by the Wizards of the Coast TTRPG and the show Critical Role.
You can choose from among your favourite CR characters and villains to fight; change levels, statistics, and starting positions; and play out different scenarios by choosing different Teams for your combatants.
This is a work in progress; there will be bugs and mechanics that don't fully work as intended.
Feedback is welcome via Twitter, and you can check out the source code on Github
